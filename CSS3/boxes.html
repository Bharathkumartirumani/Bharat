CRICKETERS

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who plays for India national cricket team. He was born in Delhi, India on November 5, 1988. Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018.

Rohot Sharma

Rohit Sharma was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra on 30 April 1987 (age 32). This right-handed batsman has scored more than 14000 runs with the help of 39 centuries and 3 double centuries in International cricket. Rohit Sharma was married to Ritika Sajdeh in 2015.

M.S.Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is one of the most well-known cricketers in the world. Successful cricketers in Indian history. He holds several records, including most runs scored in International Cricket Council (ICC) tournaments, most wickets taken by captains in Tests, and the fastest half-century in ODIs.
